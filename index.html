About

Welcome to Online Blood Donation System, a platform designed to streamline and facilitate blood donation and request

About us

Project Mission

Project mission is to bridge the gap between blood donors, seekers, and hospital, ensuring quick and efficient access to life-saving donations

Blood Donors

with this system donors can easily register, request to donate, as well as request blood, and receive approval from hospital administrators. Once the donation is approved donor can print a donation certificate

Blood Seekers

Seekers can register, log in, and request blood, which is then reviewed and approved or reject by the hospital

System Solution

This project is developed to enhace efficiency, reduce delays, and provide a paper-based yet well-organized approach to blood donation system. My goal is to save lives by making blood donation and request more accessible and management

Services

My project aimed to make blood donation more accessible, efficient, and life-saving for those in need. The system provide the following services

Blood Donor Registration

Donors can sign up, log in, and request to donate. Upon approval by the hospital, they can proceed with the donation and print a donation certificate

Blood Request Management

Blood seekers can register, log in, and send blood requests. The hospital administration reviews and approves or rejects requests based on availability.

Hospital Administration Management

The hospital can manage blood donation requests, approve or reject blood requests, and oversee available blood stock, donors, and seekers.

Donor and Seeker Interaction

Donors and seekers can efficiently communicate through the system to ensure smooth coordination for blood donation.
